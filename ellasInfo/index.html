About Me

I'm Elena, I am 36 years old and I am comming from Moldova, but above all I am: Woman, Mother, Child and of course a Human. > I was born in May, which means I love flowers and spring, I love happy companies and open minded people. I appreciate personalities with character, with principles, with dignity, as well as those who have a well-developed sense of humor.
As I mentioned above, I am a Mother and I have been raising my children for some time absolutely alone, which has changed fundamental my character and personality. And that's exactly what motivated me almost 6 years ago to emigrate from my home country - to Germany, and this decision was one of the best decisions since then.
Moldova - Despite the fact that Moldova is a small country, located between Romania and Ukraine, it hosts a society with many beautiful qualities and traditions preserved over time. To find out more about my home country you can access the link below

Germany - in 2017 Germany has adopted us as orphans and given us all this stability and an opportunity for a different future. Here I met beautiful people both face and soul. Here I discovered that "I can't"- it doesn't exist and if the Motivation and Desire is high - you can achieve success.
